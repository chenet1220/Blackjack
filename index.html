Blackjack Game

How to Play & What to Know!

THE GOAL of Blackjack is to achieve a total of 21 or Blackjack through the card values without going over 21. Going over 21 results in an automatic loss!

START GAME - Place your bet amount, and both the Player and Dealer will receive 2 cards each.

PLAYER'S HAND - You will receive 2 cards face up.

DEALER'S HAND - The Dealer will have 1 card face up and 1 card face down.

CHOICES - Choose whether to Hit (add a card) or Stand (end your turn and keep your current hand).

END TURN - Once you stand, the Dealer will reveal their hidden card and play their turn.

WINNER - The winner is whoever has Blackjack, a total closer to 21, or does not go over 21 (Ace plus a 10 is an automatic Blackjack).

TIE GAME - If both players have the same total, including 21, it is a Push (Tie Game). No winner!

CARD VALUES - Number cards hold their face value, and Face cards (J, Q, K) are worth 10.
